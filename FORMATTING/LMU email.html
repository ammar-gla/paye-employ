London Labour Market Update Jan 2023

The latest data showed little substantial change as London's labour market recovery continues to slow.

The number of payrolled employees rose slightly on the month and is now 3.1% higher than the pre-pandemic peak. The employment and inactivity rates are virtually unchanged on the quarter.

The unemployment rate rose slightly on the quarter, while the claimant count rate also appears to be on the rise.

While the inflation rate fell slightly and employee nominal wage growth remained strong, year-on-year real pay is down 1.5%.
